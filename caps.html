Welcome to CAPS

At Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS), we provide support for mental health and well-being to help you thrive as a student.

Services Offered

  • Individual Counseling
  • Group Therapy
  • Workshops
  • Crisis Support
  • Peer Support Groups
